#+title: Reverse Number Keys

When this minor mode is enabled, it reverses the number keys:


*with reverse number keys disabled*
|-------------+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---|
| key         | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8 | 9 | 0 |
| shift + key | ! | @ | # | $ | % | Ë† | & | * | ( | ) |
|-------------+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---|

*with reverse number keys enabled*
|-------------+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---|
| shift + key | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8 | 9 | 0 |
| key         | ! | @ | # | $ | % | Ë† | & | * | ( | ) |
|-------------+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---+---|

To access the numbers, press shift + key, to access the symbols, press the key.
